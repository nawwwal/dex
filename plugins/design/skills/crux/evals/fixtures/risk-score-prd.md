# Risk Score PRD Excerpt

RiskOps reviewers currently see a payment risk score, a short reason list, and a recommended action. The team believes reviewers will trust the score more if the system adds AI recommendations.

Target behavior: reviewers approve low-risk payments faster and escalate high-risk payments with clearer notes.

Known evidence:

- Sales heard from three customers that "AI explanations would be useful."
- Support says reviewers often ask what changed since the last score.
- No team has measured whether explanations change approval, escalation, or override behavior.

Open decision: add AI recommendations to the review drawer or keep the existing score with clearer change history.
