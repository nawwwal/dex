# Bluff and Slop Firewall

Ban or rewrite output that hides weak design under fashionable language.

## Metaphor slop

Ban or rewrite:

- Metaphor names without mechanics
- Quiet Sentinel
- Health Weather
- Permissions Room
- Magic Dashboard
- Copilot for X
- Command Center unless it is actually command-driven
- Cockpit unless it has instrumented controls and high-density monitoring
- Radar unless it actually maps moving risk or proximity
- Garden unless it involves growth, care, decay, and time
- Doctor unless it diagnoses, prescribes, and monitors recovery
- AI chatbot unless conversation changes the action model

## Visual slop

Ban or rewrite:

- Purple-blue gradients by default
- Glassmorphism by default
- Nested cards
- Identical card grids
- Every button primary
- Flat type hierarchy
- Tiny gray text
- Generic icon tiles
- Decorative sparklines
- Fake metrics
- Centered everything
- Modal abuse
- Redundant copy
- Motion without meaning
- Dark mode by default to look cool

## Copy slop

Ban or rewrite:

- Seamless
- Unlock
- Supercharge
- Transform
- Next-gen
- Effortless
- Powerful
- All-in-one
- Built for modern teams
- Your data, your way
- Something went wrong
- Submit
- Click here
- Learn more, when the next step is specific

## Translation rule

Aesthetic terms are allowed only if translated into execution.

Bad:
"Make it premium."

Good:
"Use fewer colors, stronger type contrast, quieter borders, more whitespace around primary actions, slower motion, and specific copy. The observable effect should be calm confidence, not luxury decoration."
