# Outside View

Reads all public-facing vault artifacts and writes a portrait from a new teammate's perspective.

## Step 1: Read Public Artifacts
- work/agent-marketplace/index.md and work/ project indexes
- memory/decisions.md (what choices were made)
- Sessions (what was worked on, in what order)
- career/case.md (what claims are being made)

## Step 2: Write the Portrait
Answer: "If I were a new teammate who read all of this, what would I think?"

Cover:
- What is this person building / working on?
- What do they seem to value? (from decisions)
- What are their blind spots? (from what's missing)
- What's impressive vs. what might concern a senior reviewer?
- Does the promotion case match the actual work?

## Step 3: Specific Gaps
Identify: what would a new teammate NOT know from reading these artifacts?
What's implied but never written down?
What claim is made without supporting evidence?

## Output Format
Write as if describing the user to someone who's never met him. First person ("This person...").
Be honest, not flattering. The goal is calibration, not validation.
