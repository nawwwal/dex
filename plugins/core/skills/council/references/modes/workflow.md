# Workflow Mode

Use when the investigation is about process, review flow, handoffs, repeated churn, operating friction, or missing automation.

## Evidence preference

Prefer:

- instructions and process docs
- recurring artifacts and templates
- handoff seams
- repeated edits or recurring confusion points

## Mode-specific lenses

Choose from these after the permanent lenses are covered.

### Bottlenecks

- Focus on: where work stalls, queues build, or review loops stretch out
- Do not duplicate: ownership analysis

### Handoff gaps

- Focus on: where information is lost between people, roles, or phases
- Do not duplicate: blind-spot work

### Repeat churn

- Focus on: work that gets redone, re-explained, or reopened
- Do not duplicate: action planning

### Automation gaps

- Focus on: repetitive steps that should be encoded in hooks, templates, or skills
- Do not duplicate: bottleneck diagnosis

## Good fit examples

- workflow fragility audits
- process bottleneck reviews
- recurring pain-point clustering
- missing-automation investigations
