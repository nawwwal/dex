# Career Framework — PD II (L2)

## Impact

A PD II is a strong design executor and intermediate product solver. They take part ownership of product solutioning alongside complete accountability for great design outcomes on their projects. Impact at this level is focused on delivering user-friendly solutions for features or small products with validated problem statements, where the design solution goes beyond functionality to address behavioral and visceral quality.

They independently drive design from research through handoff — scoping the problem, exploring multiple directions, covering 95–100% of use and edge cases, and delivering well-crafted presentations that drive stakeholder alignment. Their work demonstrates measurable craft quality (Blade Score, iteration depth, prototype fidelity) and is validated through hallway testing or user testing before shipping.

## Leadership

A PD II contributes to the growth of individual designers and team culture. They share constructive feedback to help other designers improve, and buddy new designers joining the team. They contribute to team-wide activities with guidance — external talks, town halls, offsites, all-hands.

They drive buy-in for their design ideas with stakeholders through well-crafted presentations and are capable of running design discussions or alignment sessions with wider forums. They mentor by example — demonstrating the level of design craft, ownership, and user-centricity expected at PD II and building toward PD III.
