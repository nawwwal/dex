    WaitApproval -->|вң… Approved| P5_Start

    Remind -->|Send reminder| SendDM
    Remind -->|Escalate| EscalateSlack[DM to AD Research:<br/>Pingal or Varghese]

    Remind -->|Wait longer| WaitApproval

    SheetError -->|Success| ConfirmLog[вң… Confirm row # to user]
    Fallback --> SendStatus
    ConfirmLog --> SendStatus

    SendStatus[Send Status Summary<br/>to Saurav Rastogi<br/>рҹҺҜ Study details +<br/>screener + agency POC]

    SendStatus --> FinalConfirm[вң… Confirm to user:<br/>Next steps, timeline,<br/>preparation tips]

    FinalConfirm --> Done([Workflow Complete])
